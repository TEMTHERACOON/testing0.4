How Can GenAI Adapt and Personalize Studies?

  • It acts as a powerful learning partner providing personalized, adaptive, and supportive guidance.
  • It helps students understand complex concepts through clear explanations while identifying errors and suggesting improvements.
  • It creates a more individualized learning environment, allowing students to learn at their own pace and style.

How to use GenAI in making Knowledge Construction More Interactive.

  • Strengthen programming, reading, writing, analyzing, and additional skills with GenAI tools.
  • Break down complex concepts into simpler explanations.
  • Test and improve certain activities such as coding and writing with AI feedback and error detection.
  • Receive real-time hints and guidance during learning challenges.

How Can Using GenAI Transform Your Learning Habits?

Many Computer Studies students view AI as helpful for gaining quick feedback and customizing lessons creatively and efficiently. However.... Dependency can lead to reduced critical thinking and appreciation for real learning.



How to Turn Bad AI Habits Into Good Ones:


  • Be cautious and intentional with your AI usage.
  • Use AI as a guide, not as a replacement for your learning process.
  • Treat AI as a supportive tool that enhances your study journey.
